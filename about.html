About Me

Chelsea is multicultural and multifaceted in design, with a passion for travel and culinary experiences. After traveling the world with sports and an extremely decorated athletic journey, she began her career as a professional firefighter with Long Beach Fire Department. Having been a dual-sport NCAA Division I Athlete, Chelsea was able to establish an exceptional work ethic and quickly learned to manage working in high stress environments.

Through her career at Long Beach Fire Department, Chelsea has worked at the busiest fire stations within the city, as well as on one of the busiest resources in the nation. She has been a part of multiple incidents where she diligently worked to protect life, property, and the environment from fire and other emergencies. She has responded to various hazardous materials incidents, as well as, multiple natural disasters due to severe wildfires within California. She has also established exemplary skill in medically treating patients with advanced life saving measures in some of the highest poverty and crime related areas within the city of Long Beach. Her attention to detail in high stress situations is bar none. Chelsea is currently working to serve the community that helped her grow and excel in multiple facets.

Concurrently, Chelsea is enrolled in a Data Science Bootcamp with TripleTen, where she is learning to apply her analytical skills to solve real-world problems using data. Chelsea is excited to leverage her unique background and experiences in her new career path, aiming to make a positive impact through data-driven solutions.
